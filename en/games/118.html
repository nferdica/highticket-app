About The Game

Cash Show is the first-ever luxury crash game, developed in partnership with Fashion TV Gaming Group. In this exciting fusion of fashion, thrill, and chance, players bet before the real model takes the stage and starts walking. 

The objective is to predict when the model will stop walking and cash out before she does.

Features

Provable Fairness
To assure true randomness, we only use the most reliable and high-end Random Number Generator technologies. Players can check the fairness of the game by using Hash Code system.
Two Bets
Players can make two different bets in the same round.
Half Cashout
By clicking the ”Half Cashout” button, the players can get half of their winnings and continue playing while using the rest of the earnings.
Unique Game Concept
Cash Show provides an experience of a private luxury fashion show, with real-life FashionTV models taking centre stage, where you get to determine their next move.
Bonus System
FreeBet and FreeAmount bonuses provide an option to award bonuses to the players, with terms set by the operator.
Auto Cashout
When using the Auto Cashout function, the player pre-defines the odds they want to cash out at, enabling them to control the amount of risk they want to take with each bet.
