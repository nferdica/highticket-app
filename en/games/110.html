About The Game

Hi Lo is an easy yet engaging game where the player must guess if the next card is going to be higher or lower than the previous one. As the streak of correct guesses extends, so do the potential rewards. 

Offering the flexibility to join the game whenever, it ensures consistent player engagement and continuous thrill.
