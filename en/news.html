We’re Expanding Our Fast Games Portfolio With Four New Launches
We are proud to announce the release of four new game titles that will be showcased at the upcoming SAGSE Latam Expo in Buenos Aires. Senior Vice President of Sales and Business Development Gil Soffer commented, “We continue to consolidate our positioning in the market with fast and skill games and tools that appeal to digital players looking for something new and exciting but simultaneously provide our partners with revenue opportunities.” The new game releases include: Jungle Wheel – a game with three different-sized wheels placed on each other. Each wheel consists of 5 sectors of different colours and the goal is to have the matching colour combination for the highlighted sector on the top of the wheel. Ninja Crash – a turbo-type game where the player needs to slice the cakes that bounce on the screen by swiping over them or clicking on them. The aim of the game is to cut the cakes, thus getting winning odds. Crasher – a high-stakes game where players place bets before the game starts, then wait until the Crasher hits the ground and the crack begins to grow. And finally, in Roulette X, the player must predict the number where the ball will land after spinning on the roulette wheel and place one or more bets on that number. Galaxsys is dedicated to delivering innovative and engaging gaming experiences that attract new players and provide revenue opportunities for our partners. We are constantly expanding our portfolio with new and exciting games, such as Jungle Wheel, Ninja Crash, Crasher, and Roulette X.
