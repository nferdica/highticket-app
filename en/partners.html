Collaboration with Fashion TV Gaming Group

We are proud to have a collaboration with Fashion TV Gaming Group, a partnership that has resulted in two unique games infused with a touch of fashion elegance - Cash Show and F Mines.

Cash Show: The first-ever luxury Fast Game that not only promises a thrilling gaming experience but also features real-life models, adding a touch of glamour and sophistication to the gameplay.

F Mines: This turbo game offers an exciting blend of strategy and risk, providing a unique and engaging experience for players. To win the game, the players need to find as many diamonds as possible without coming across any dynamites.
